Chicken Corn Soup

Description

Chicken corn soup is a comforting and flavorful dish made from a base of chicken broth, shredded chicken, sweet corn, and often eggs for a silky texture. Commonly seasoned with ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and white pepper, it offers a perfect balance of savory and slightly sweet flavors. Ideal as an appetizer or light meal, it is enjoyed worldwide, particularly during colder months.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Stock - 4 cups
  • Shredded cooked chicken - 1 cup
  • Sweet Corn - 1 cup
  • Egg - 1 (beaten)
  • Cornstarch - 2 tablespoons (mixed with water)
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the Stock: In a pot, bring the chicken stock to a boil.
  2. Add Ingredients: Stir in the shredded chicken and sweet corn. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Thicken the Soup: Slowly stir in the cornstarch slurry (cornstarch mixed with water) and let the soup thicken.
  5. Add Egg: While stirring the soup, slowly pour in the beaten egg to create silky egg strands.
  6. Serve: Pour into bowls, garnish with black pepper or spring onions (optional), and serve hot!
