Lentil Soup

Description

Lentil soup is a hearty and nutritious dish made from lentils, vegetables, and aromatic spices. It is a protein-packed, plant-based meal that is both satisfying and healthy, perfect for any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Dry lentils - 1 cup
  • Carrot - 1 (chopped)
  • Celery - 1 stalk (chopped)
  • Onion - 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic - 2 cloves (minced)
  • Vegetable broth - 4 cups
  • Cumin - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a pot, saut√© onion, garlic, carrot, and celery until softened.
  3. Add lentils, vegetable broth, and cumin to the pot.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and blend part of the soup for a creamier texture if desired.
  6. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon and crusty bread.
