Tomato Soup

Description

Tomato soup is a classic comfort food made from ripe tomatoes, blended to a smooth consistency. It is often seasoned with herbs like basil or thyme and can be served with cream for added richness. This warm and tangy soup pairs well with crusty bread or a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes - 4 cups (chopped)
  • Onion - 1 (chopped)
  • Garlic - 2 cloves (minced)
  • Vegetable broth - 3 cups
  • Cream - 1/2 cup (optional)
  • Salt and pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Blend the mixture until smooth using a blender or immersion blender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and stir in cream if desired.
  6. Serve hot with bread or croutons.
